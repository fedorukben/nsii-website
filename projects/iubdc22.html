Inter-University Big Data Challenge 2022

NSII entered a team into the 2022 Inter-University Big Data Challenge hosted by STEM Fellowship. The team consisted of Neel Shah, Isaac Bahler and Samantha Chong. The team won the 1st place Scientific Communications Award, winning $1000.00 and a waived APF at STEM Fellowship Journal. The team is currently pursuing publication of their work.
